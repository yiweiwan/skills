# Presentation Builder

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, decision to drive, time limit, presenter, and venue.
2. Define the narrative arc before designing individual slides.
3. Keep one main idea per slide.
4. Use titles that state the takeaway, not just the topic.
5. Put supporting details in appendix slides when they interrupt the main flow.

## Slide Deck Scope

- Executive update: status, metrics, wins, risks, decisions, next steps.
- Training: objective, concept, example, practice, checklist, resources.
- Use `pitch-deck-builder` for investor, sales, partnership, or product pitch decks.
- Do not write full speaker scripts here; use `speaker-notes`.
- Do not develop product requirements here; use `product-requirements`.
- Do not synthesize source research here; use `research-synthesis`.

## Verification

- Check that the deck can be understood from slide titles alone.
- Confirm numbers, labels, source notes, and action requests.
