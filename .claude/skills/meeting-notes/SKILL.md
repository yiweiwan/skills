# Meeting Notes

## Workflow

1. Identify meeting purpose, date, attendees, and desired output style.
2. Separate discussion, decisions, action items, owners, due dates, risks, and open questions.
3. Preserve exact decisions and commitments.
4. Convert vague follow-ups into explicit action items when evidence supports it.
5. Flag missing owners or due dates.

## Output Pattern

- Summary.
- Decisions.
- Action items.
- Risks or blockers.
- Open questions.

## Verification

- Check names, dates, owners, deadlines, and decision wording.
- Do not invent attendance, ownership, or deadlines.
