# Stakeholder Update

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, update period, project goal, and decisions needed.
2. Lead with current status and whether attention is needed.
3. Separate progress, risks, blockers, decisions, and next steps.
4. State owners and dates for action items.
5. Keep detail proportional to stakeholder needs.

## Structure

- Status.
- Since last update.
- Risks and blockers.
- Decisions needed.
- Next steps.

## Verification

- Check dates, metrics, owners, and decision requests.
- Make risk severity and urgency clear.
