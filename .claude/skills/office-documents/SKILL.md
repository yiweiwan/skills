# Office Documents

## Workflow

1. Identify the exact document type.
2. Switch to a narrower skill whenever one applies.
3. Use this skill only for mixed document tasks or initial classification.
4. Preserve names, dates, headings, numbering, and source facts.
5. Flag legal, medical, financial, HR, privacy, or compliance review needs.
