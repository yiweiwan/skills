# Research Synthesis

## Workflow

1. Clarify the research question, decision context, audience, and confidence needs.
2. Identify whether the task is web research, competitor analysis, document summarization, literature review, or mixed synthesis.
3. Route to a narrower skill whenever possible.
4. Separate facts, source claims, analysis, assumptions, and recommendations.
5. Synthesize into decision-ready output.

## Prefer Narrow Skills

- Current web research: `web-research`.
- Competitor or product comparison: `competitor-analysis`.
- Provided files, notes, transcripts, or long documents: `document-summarization`.
- Academic papers or technical literature: `literature-review`.

## Verification

- Include citations or source references when sources were used.
- Check that conclusions follow from evidence.
- Flag what would change the answer or need follow-up research.
