# Runbook Writing

## Workflow

1. Identify service, symptom, alert, severity, owner, and operational context.
2. Define quick triage steps before deep diagnosis.
3. Write diagnosis, mitigation, rollback, escalation, and verification steps.
4. Include commands, dashboards, logs, and safety warnings only when accurate.
5. Keep irreversible or risky operations explicit.

## Structure

- Symptoms and alert source.
- Impact and severity.
- Quick checks.
- Diagnosis.
- Mitigation.
- Rollback.
- Escalation.
- Verification.

## Verification

- Check commands, dashboard links, owners, and thresholds.
- Mark assumptions and environment-specific steps.
