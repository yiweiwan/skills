# Speaker Notes

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, presenter, time limit, tone, and desired level of scripting.
2. Read the slide title and slide content before writing notes.
3. Write notes that explain the slide's takeaway, not a duplicate of slide bullets.
4. Add transitions between slides when the deck needs live flow.
5. Keep timing realistic and mark optional details.

## Style

- Use natural spoken language.
- Keep sentences short enough to deliver aloud.
- Mark emphasis, pause, demo cues, and audience questions only when useful.
- Do not rewrite the deck structure unless asked.

## Verification

- Check that the notes match the slide order and message.
- Confirm timing fits the presentation length.
- Remove unsupported claims and unverified numbers.
