# SOP Writing

## Workflow

1. Identify process owner, operator, trigger, inputs, outputs, and success criteria.
2. Write steps in execution order.
3. Include prerequisites, tools, permissions, quality checks, and escalation paths.
4. Separate normal path from exceptions.
5. Make each step observable and testable.

## Style

- Use imperative verbs.
- Keep one action per step.
- Avoid policy explanations unless needed for execution.

## Verification

- Check that a new operator could follow the SOP without hidden context.
- Confirm edge cases, rollback, and escalation are visible.
