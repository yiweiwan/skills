# Competitor Analysis

## Workflow

1. Identify the comparison goal, buyer/user, market segment, geography, and decision criteria.
2. Define competitors or options and gather comparable evidence.
3. Compare only criteria that matter to the decision.
4. Separate observable facts from interpretation.
5. Recommend the best fit for the stated context, not a generic winner.

## Comparison Criteria

- Target customer and positioning.
- Core features and workflow fit.
- Pricing and packaging.
- Integrations, ecosystem, trust, compliance, and support.
- Strengths, weaknesses, risks, and switching costs.

## Verification

- Cite current source pages for pricing, features, and claims.
- Mark unknowns and assumptions.
- Check that the recommendation follows from the criteria.
