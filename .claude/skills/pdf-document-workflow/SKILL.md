# PDF Document Workflow

## Workflow

1. Identify whether the PDF is text-based, scanned, form-based, or image-heavy.
2. Extract text while preserving page numbers and section context when possible.
3. Separate extracted facts from interpretation.
4. For scanned PDFs, note OCR uncertainty and review important numbers manually.
5. Produce summaries, action lists, or converted drafts according to the user's requested output.

## Rules

- Preserve page references for quotes, findings, and obligations.
- Do not claim exact formatting preservation unless verified.
- Flag unreadable text, missing pages, signatures, stamps, and tables that may need manual review.

## Verification

- Spot-check extracted text against representative pages.
- Confirm page counts and important dates, names, totals, and clauses.
