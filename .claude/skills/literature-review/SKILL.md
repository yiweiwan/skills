# Literature Review

## Workflow

1. Clarify research question, domain, inclusion criteria, and desired depth.
2. Identify papers, publication venues, dates, authors, methods, datasets, and claims.
3. Compare methods, evidence quality, assumptions, limitations, and results.
4. Group papers by themes, approaches, or findings.
5. Summarize consensus, disagreement, gaps, and practical implications.

## Review Focus

- Research question and contribution.
- Methodology and dataset.
- Results and limitations.
- Relevance to the user's decision.
- Reproducibility and evidence strength.

## Verification

- Cite papers or source identifiers when used.
- Avoid overstating claims beyond the paper evidence.
- Flag missing full text, weak evidence, or unclear methodology.
