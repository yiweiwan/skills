# Policy Writing

## Workflow

1. Identify policy owner, audience, scope, authority, and review cadence.
2. Define purpose, scope, rules, responsibilities, exceptions, enforcement, and related documents.
3. Use precise obligations: must, must not, may, should.
4. Separate policy requirements from procedures.
5. Flag legal, HR, finance, privacy, and compliance review needs.

## Style

- Use consistent terms and defined roles.
- Avoid ambiguous language such as "as needed" without criteria.
- Keep exceptions explicit and auditable.

## Verification

- Check scope, effective dates, owners, approvals, and contradictions.
- Confirm requirements are enforceable and reviewable.
