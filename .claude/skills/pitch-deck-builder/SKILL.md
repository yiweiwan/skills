# Pitch Deck Builder

## Workflow

1. Identify pitch audience, stage, objective, meeting length, and desired ask.
2. Build the story around problem, insight, solution, proof, and next step.
3. Choose only slides that support the pitch goal.
4. Make the ask explicit and proportional to the evidence.
5. Keep claims defensible and mark assumptions.

## Common Slide Order

- Title and one-line positioning.
- Problem and affected customer.
- Insight or market shift.
- Solution and product proof.
- Market, business model, traction, competition, team, ask.

## Verification

- Check that every slide supports the ask.
- Confirm numbers, sources, and claims are defensible.
- Remove generic filler and unsupported market statements.
